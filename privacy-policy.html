Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

AI Notes Maker is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best study experience:

  • Study Content: Topics and text you input for note generation are processed locally in your browser
  • Saved Notes: Your generated notes are stored locally on your device using browser storage
  • Usage Analytics: Basic usage statistics to improve our service (anonymized)
  • Device Information: Browser type, screen resolution for optimal display

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Generate personalized study materials based on your topics
  • Save your notes locally for future access
  • Improve the AI note generation algorithms
  • Provide technical support when needed
  • Enhance user experience and website functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize your data security:

  • Local Storage: Your notes are stored locally on your device, not on our servers
  • No Account Required: You can use our service without creating an account
  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not use cookies for advertising purposes

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all data stored locally on your device
  • Delete: Clear all saved notes using the "Clear All" button
  • Export: Download your notes as PDF files
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics by using browser settings

6. Children's Privacy

AI Notes Maker is safe for students of all ages:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No account creation or personal details required
  • Parents can monitor and control their child's usage
  • Educational content is appropriate for all age groups

7. Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (privacy-friendly implementation)
  • jsPDF Library: For PDF generation (runs locally in your browser)
  • No Social Media Tracking: Social links do not track your activity

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally:

  • Changes will be posted on this page with updated date
  • Significant changes will be highlighted on the main website
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of updated terms

9. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@ainotesmaker.com
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours
  • Data Protection Officer: Available for EU users

Summary: AI Notes Maker respects your privacy. We store your notes locally on your device, use minimal tracking, and never sell your data. You have full control over your information at all times.
